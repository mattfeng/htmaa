# diodes

## semiconductor basics
- the _(signal) diode_ is the most basic _active component_
- diode have an _exponential i-V_ relationship
- **resistivity ($\rho$).** measured in <C chem="\pu{\Omega m^{-1}}" />
    - comparing $\rho$ lets us define _conductors_, _semi-conductors_, and _insulators_
    - conductors ($10^{-6}$)
    - insulators ($10^6$)
